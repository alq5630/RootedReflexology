About Me

Hi, I’m Nicole, and I’ve been practicing Reflexology since 2016. With a background in Health Sciences, I began my Reflexology journey by completing a 150-hour certification program with Tracy McGovern at Essential Connections. Soon after, I earned nationally certified through the American Reflexology Certification Board (ARCB).

I’m committed to ongoing learning and have explored a variety of addtional techniques, including Hand, Ear, and Facial Reflexology. I also completed a 25-hour course through CARE, where I trained in Raindrop Therapy, Vita Flex, the chemistry of essential oils, and emotional release methods.

Over the years, I’ve seen how transformative Reflexology can be in reducing stress, easing pain, supporting mental health, and bringing balance to the nervous system. It's a priviledge to share this gentle, holistic practice and help clients discover greater wellness in their daily lives.
